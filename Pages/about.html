Who We Are

Crème Yogurt is a new company that offers a range of both dairy and non-dairy Yogurts for sale. The owner of the company is lactose intolerant but loves Yogurt, so she started her own brand of Yogurts that everyone can love and enjoy. As this is a fairly new company still finding itself, the needs of the website requested are not complex.

The main purpose for the website is to be a source of information for customers as to what is available. Crème Yogurt is constantly trying new formulas and putting them out to the market, and a website would be a great way to provide interested persons with the latest and available options for Yogurts from Crème Yogurt.
